Before adding any enhancement, ask:

1. **Does it simplify or complicate?**
   - ‚úÖ Simplifies ‚Üí Keep
   - ‚ùå Complicates ‚Üí Remove or simplify

2. **Is the message clear?**
   - ‚úÖ Clear ‚Üí Keep
   - ‚ùå Confusing ‚Üí Simplify or remove

3. **Does it drive action?**
   - ‚úÖ Actionable ‚Üí Keep
   - ‚ùå Just informational ‚Üí Remove

4. **Can we measure it?**
   - ‚úÖ Measurable ‚Üí Keep
   - ‚ùå Vague ‚Üí Remove or clarify
