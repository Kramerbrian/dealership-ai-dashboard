# Your Vercel Domain Verification Token

vc-domain-verify=dealershipai.com,b6d0acdf14a0e0348f56

## Add this TXT record in Squarespace:

Type: TXT
Host: _vercel
Value: vc-domain-verify=dealershipai.com,b6d0acdf14a0e0348f56
TTL: 3600 (or default)

## Step-by-step:

1. Squarespace is now open in your browser
2. Click on: dealershipai.com
3. Click: Advanced Settings
4. Click: DNS Settings
5. Click: Add Record
6. Select Type: TXT
7. Host: _vercel
8. Value: Copy the verification string above
9. Click: Save

## After saving, run this command to monitor:

./scripts/check-domain-verification.sh

## Once TXT record is detected, run:

./scripts/complete-domain-setup.sh
